Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 24, 2026

PingFlow ("we", "us", or "our") provides WhatsApp chatbot automation services to local businesses. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when our clients use our services and when their customers interact with our WhatsApp bots.

1. Information We Collect

When customers interact with a WhatsApp chatbot powered by PingFlow on behalf of a client business, we may process the following data:

  • WhatsApp phone numbers of end users
  • Message content sent to the chatbot
  • Names provided voluntarily during conversations
  • Appointment details and product inquiries submitted through the bot

2. How We Use the Information

We use this information solely to:

  • Operate and deliver the chatbot service to the client business
  • Process appointment bookings and customer queries on behalf of clients
  • Log conversation data in client-owned Google Sheets for business operations
  • Improve service quality and bot performance

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent personal data to third parties. Data collected through the WhatsApp chatbot is shared only with:

  • The client business on whose behalf the chatbot operates
  • AI processing services (such as Groq) to generate bot responses — no personally identifiable information is stored by these services beyond the active session
  • Meta Platforms, as required to operate through the WhatsApp Business API

4. Data Storage and Retention

Conversation logs are stored in Google Sheets managed by the client business. PingFlow does not maintain its own long-term database of end-user messages. Clients are responsible for managing and deleting their own data.

5. WhatsApp and Meta Platform Data

PingFlow operates through the Meta WhatsApp Business API. All use of WhatsApp Platform Data complies with Meta's Platform Terms and WhatsApp Business Policy. We only access message data necessary to operate the chatbot service.

6. User Data Deletion

If you are an end user who interacted with a PingFlow-powered chatbot and wish to request deletion of your data, please contact us at contact@pingflow.in. We will coordinate with the relevant client business to process your request within 30 days.

7. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data processed through our services. Access to client data is restricted to authorized personnel only.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised date. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
